About Us

Advanced Healthcare
Rooted in Kurnool

Agraseni Hospital provides exceptional standards of healthcare supported by advanced technology. Situated in A Camp, Kurnool, our hospital boasts a well-equipped clinic outfitted with state-of-the-art facilities with distinct waiting and consultation areas ensuring comfort at every step.

Our clinic is led by specialized physicians proficient in Critical Care, 24/7 Intensive Care, Nephrology, Orthopedics, Minimally Invasive Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Urology, Plastic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Trauma Care, and more.
